canon: Nag-Hammadi
source_type: Gnostic-Codex
translation: Isenberg
id: G-PHILIP-6
---

Those who sow in winter reap in summer. The winter is the world, the summer the other Aeon (eternal realm). Let us sow in the world that we may reap in the summer. Because of this, it is fitting for us not to pray in the winter. After the winter comes the summer. If any man reaps in winter he will not actually reap but only pluck out, since it will not provide work for him. Not only does it [...] come forth, but it is not [...] on the Sabbath [...] is fruitless.
