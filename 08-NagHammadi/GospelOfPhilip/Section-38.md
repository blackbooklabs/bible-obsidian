The forms of evil spirit include male ones and female ones. The male ones are those which unite with the souls which inhabit a female form, but the female ones are those which are mingled with those in a male form, though one who is a disobedient. And no one shall be able to escape them, since they detain him if he does not receive a male power or a female power, the bridegroom and the bride. One receives them from the mirrored bridal chamber. When the wanton women see a male sitting alone, they leap down upon him and play with him and defile him. So also the lewd men when they see a beautiful woman sitting alone, they persuade her and compel her, wishing to defile her. But if they see the man and his wife sitting beside one another, the female cannot come in to the man, nor can the male come in to the woman. So if the image and the angel are united with one another, neither can any venture to go in to the male or the female.
