"Jesus" is a hidden name, "Christ" is a revealed name. For this reason "Jesus" is not particular to any language; rather he is always called by the name "Jesus." While as for "Christ," in Syriac it is "Messias," in Greek it is "Christos." Certainly all the others have it according to their own language. "The Nazarene" is he who reveals what is hidden.
