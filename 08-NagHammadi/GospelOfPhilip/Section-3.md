Those who are heirs to the dead are themselves dead, and they inherit the dead. Those who are heirs to what is living are themselves living, and they are heirs to both the living and the dead. The dead are heirs to nothing. For how can he who is dead inherit? If he who is dead inherits what is living he will not die, but he who is dead will live even more.
