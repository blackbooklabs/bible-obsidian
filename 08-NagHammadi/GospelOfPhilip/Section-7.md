source_type: Gnostic-Codex

id: G-PHILIP-7
---

Christ came to ransom some, to save others, to redeem others. He ransomed those who were strangers and made them his own. And he set his own apart, those whom he gave as a pledge according to his plan. It was not only when he appeared that he voluntarily laid down his life, but he laid it down from the very day the world came into being. Then he came first in order to take it, since it had been given as a pledge. It was in the hands of robbers and had been taken captive, but he saved it. He redeemed the good people in the world as well as the evil.
