Before Christ came, there was no bread in the world, just as Paradise, the place where Adam was, had many trees to afford food for the animals, but no wheat to sustain man. Man used to feed like the animals, but when Christ came, the perfect man, he brought bread from heaven in order that man might be nourished with the food of man.
