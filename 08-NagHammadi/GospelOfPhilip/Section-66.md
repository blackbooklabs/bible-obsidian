An ass which turns a millstone did a hundred miles walking. When it was loosed, it found that it was still at the same place. There are men who make many journeys, but make no progress towards any destination. When evening comes for them, they see neither city nor village, neither human creation nor nature, power nor angel. In vain have the wretches labored.
