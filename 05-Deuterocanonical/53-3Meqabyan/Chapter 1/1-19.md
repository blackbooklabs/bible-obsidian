"'And let those who refused me, who did not err because of me, who did not keep my command, but who did Your Command, fulfilled Your Will, and kept Your Word—let them belong to Your Lordship. Since they did not err as I misled them, having refused what I taught them—when You loved me, let them now take the crown You once gave to me.'"
