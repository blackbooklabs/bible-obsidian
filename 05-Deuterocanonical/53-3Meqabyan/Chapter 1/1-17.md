"When He increased His anger against me, and when He commanded that I be bound and cast into Gehenna—when my Creator commanded this—I interceded with my Lord. I pleaded before Him, saying: 'As You are angry with me, and as You have admonished me by Your punishment and chastised me by Your wrath—Lord, my Lord, grant me a delay that I might speak one thing before You.'"
