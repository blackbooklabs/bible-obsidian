Will you say, ‘I wear iron clothes, so spears and bows cannot hurt me’? The Lord who honors Israel said: "It is not by spears that I will take revenge and destroy you." He told him, "I shall bring upon you heart sickness, itching, and rheumatism—sicknesses worse and firmer than the throw of a spear or the sting of a bow. Yet even by these things, I will not finish My revenge."
