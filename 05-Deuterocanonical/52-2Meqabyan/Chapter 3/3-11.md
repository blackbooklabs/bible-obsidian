At that time, he came out of the pit and bowed to the prophet. He swore, saying: "As I have saddened the Lord, let me do what you desire; let the Lord do so to me if I separate from you. As we had no Law, I did not live firmly in His Command like my fathers. You know that our fathers taught us to worship idols."
