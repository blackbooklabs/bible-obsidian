But the beliefs of the Samaritans and the Jews—the Sadducees who do not believe the dead will rise, and the Pharisees—totally sadden me and trouble my reasoning. "We will die tomorrow," the Jews say. "Let us eat and drink, for we will die tomorrow; there is no peace we will see in the grave."
