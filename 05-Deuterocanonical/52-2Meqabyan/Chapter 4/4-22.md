Again he prayed that He might give his children a place in Zion and a house in Jerusalem—that He might give them a Heavenly Seed of Virtue in Zion and a Heavenly House of the Soul in Jerusalem—and that He might save him from the destruction spoken by the tongue of the prophets, accepting his repentance and the mourning he wept before the Lord.
