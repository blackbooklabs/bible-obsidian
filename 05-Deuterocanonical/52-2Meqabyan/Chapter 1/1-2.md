Because the Syrians, the Edomites, and the Amalekites were united with the Moabite man, Meqabees, who destroyed the land of Jerusalem—for they camped from Samaria to the square of Jerusalem and throughout its entire region—they killed in war, sparing no one except a few people who fled.
