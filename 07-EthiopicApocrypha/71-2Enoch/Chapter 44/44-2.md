canon: Ethiopic-81

book: 2 Enoch

---

Whoever reviles the rulers face, and abhors the Lords face, has despised the Lord's face, and he who vents anger on any man without injury, the Lords great anger will cut him down, he who spits on the face of man reproachfully, will be cut down at the Lords great judgment.
