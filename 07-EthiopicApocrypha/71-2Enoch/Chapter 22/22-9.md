And Michael did thus, as the Lord told him. He anointed me, and dressed me, and the appearance of that ointment is more than the great light, and his ointment is like sweet dew, and its smell mild, shining like the suns ray, and I looked at myself, and (I) was like (transfigured)(http://scriptures.lds.org/bdt/trnsfgrt) one of his glorious ones.
