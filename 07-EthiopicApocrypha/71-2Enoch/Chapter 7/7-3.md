And I felt great pity for them, and they saluted me, and said to me: Man of God, pray for us to the Lord; and I answered to them: Who am I, a mortal man, that I should pray for angels (spirits)(http://scriptures.lds.org/bds/spirit)? Who knows whither I go, or what will befall me? Or who will pray for me?
