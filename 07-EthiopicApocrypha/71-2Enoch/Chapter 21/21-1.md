And theCherubim and seraphim standing about the throne, the six-winged and many-eyed ones do not depart, standing before the Lords face doing his will, and cover his whole throne, singing with gentle voice before the Lords face: Holy, holy, holy, Lord Ruler of Sabaoth, heavens and earth are full of Your glory.
