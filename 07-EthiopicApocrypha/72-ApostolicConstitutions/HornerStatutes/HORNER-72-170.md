Said  Bartholomew :  I  beseech  you,  O  my  brethren, 
while  time  remains  to  you,  and  ye  are  yourselves  able 
to  do  good  to  others  {/it.  them),  do  not  be  slack  in  giving ; 
while  anything  remains  to  you  ye  shall  give  it.  For  the 
day  of  the  Lord  is  near,  and  all  that  will  be  seen  in  it  and  10 
the  wicked  will  perish  ;  and  the  Lord  will  come,  and 
his  reward  with  him.  Be  lawgivers  to  yourselves,  be 
teachers  of  yourselves,  as  God  taught  you,  and  keep 
that  which  I  said,  and  add  not  to  them,  nor  diminish 
from  them.  15
