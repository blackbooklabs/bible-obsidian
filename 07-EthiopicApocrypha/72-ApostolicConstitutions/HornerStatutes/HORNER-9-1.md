*  Said  Natnael :  O  my  son,  thou  shalt  not  be 
a  liar,  because  lying  tends  to  theft :  nor  shalt  thou  be  a 
lover  of  gold,  nor  a  lover  of  vainglory ;  because  all  this 
is  sin,  and  leads  to  theft.
