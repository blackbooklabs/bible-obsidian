Concerning  the  ordination  of  Widows.  If 
a  widow  is  ordained  she  shall  not  be  sealed,  but  be  made 
by  the  name.  And  if  it  was  one  whose  husband  died  a 
long  time,  she  shall  be  ordained.  And  if  it  was  one 
whose  husband  had  lately  died,  she  shall  not  be  trusted.
