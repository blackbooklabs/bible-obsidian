God  the  almighty,  we  pray  thee  and  we  beseech  thee, 
cause  to  rise  the  waters  of  the  rivers  up  to  their  (proper) 
15  measure.  Make  to  rejoice  the  face  of  the  earth,  and 
water  its  furrows.  Multiply  food,  and  bless  the  circle  of 
the  year  with  thy  goodness.  Speedily  let  thy  mercy  find 
us,  O  Lord.
