If  the  concubine  of  an  unbeliever  Is  a  slave  and  kept 
herself  to  him  alone,  let  her  come  in  ;  if  she  was  defiled 
by  others,  let  her  be  sent  away.  If  a  believer  has  a  con- 
cubine and  she  stays  with  him,  let  him  desist  from  her  if  lo 
he  is  a  Christian  and  marry  her  according  to  the  law.  If 
she  is  free,  let  him  do  with  her  according  to  the  law,  and 
if  he  is  unwilling  to  do  this,  let  him  be  sent  away.
