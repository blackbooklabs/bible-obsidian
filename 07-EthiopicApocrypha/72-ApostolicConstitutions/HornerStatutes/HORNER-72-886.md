56.  Further  (de),  let  each  one  of  the  deacons  and  the 
subdeacons  attend  (pr.)  upon  the  bishop,  and  tell  him  of 
all  who  are  sick ;  that  if  it  seem  good  (dokei)  to  the  15 
bishop  he  may  visit  them.  For  let  the  sick  be  consoled 
when  they  see  the  chief  priest  visiting  them,  and  that  they 
are  remembered.
