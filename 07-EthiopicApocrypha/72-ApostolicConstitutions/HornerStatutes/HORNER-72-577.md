If   there   is  one  who    follows    the    customs  of   the 
heathen,'  or  the  fabulous  words  of  the  Jews,  let  him  is 
desist  or  be  sent  away.     One  (who)  is  mad  after  and 
gazes  at  the  theatre  or  gladiators  or  places  of  the  lions, 
if  he  desists  (let  him  come  in),  or  let  him  be  sent  away.
