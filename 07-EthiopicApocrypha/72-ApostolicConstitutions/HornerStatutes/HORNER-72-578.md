Let  them  who  begin  to  be  instructed  be  instructed  three 
years.  If  a  man  has  love  and  good-will  and  he  displays  20 
upright  life,  let  him  be  accepted,  for  the  matter  is  not 
concerned  with  time,  but  it  is  only  an  affair  of  intention 
and  conduct.  If  there  is  a  layman  teaching  in  the  words, 
and  he  is  quiet  in  manner  of  life,  let  him  teach.*  For 
they  shall  be  all  taught  of  God,  said  the  prophet  25
