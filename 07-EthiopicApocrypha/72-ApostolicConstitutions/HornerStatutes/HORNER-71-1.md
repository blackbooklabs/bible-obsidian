the  Faith.  And  those  who  flee  from  city  to  city  for  the 
Faith  and  in  remembrance  of  the  word  of  our  Lord  :  and 
they  know  that  the  spirit  is  willing  but  the  flesh  is  weak, 
and  they  flee  from  place  to  place,  and  they  patiently  endure 
being  spoiled  of  their  goods  that  they  might  keep  the  «&gt; 
name  of  Christ  and  not  deny  him ;  help  them  and  give 
them  all  that  they  desire,  and  fulfil  the  commandment  of 
God,  our  Lord.
