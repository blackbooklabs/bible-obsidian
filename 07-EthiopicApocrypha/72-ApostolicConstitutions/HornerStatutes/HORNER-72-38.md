And  again  we  beseech  the  almighty  God,  the  Father, 
JO  etc,  for  those  our  brethren  who  make  a  journey,  and  also 
for  those  who  are  about  to  make  a  journey,  whether 
by  sea,  or  by  rivers,  or  by  the  deeps,  or  by  road,  in 
whatever  journey  they  may  travel,  that  he  may  bring  all 
into  the  safe  harbour,  the  almighty  Lord  our  God. 
IS      The  deacon  shall  say  :  Pray.
