And  again  we  beseech  the  almighty  God,  the  Father, 
etc.,  for  the  blessed  and  holy  Papas,  Aba  N(egale),  that 
thou  wilt  in  keeping  keep  him  for  us  during  many  years 
and  during  times  of  peace,  while  he  fulfils  the  priesthood  ao 
which  has  been  entrusted  to  him  by  thee.  May  the 
Lord  our  God  grant  rich  favour.
