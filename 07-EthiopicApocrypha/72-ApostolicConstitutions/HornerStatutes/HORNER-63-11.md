If  there  is  a  man  who  eats  of  the  house  of  the  heretics 
and  the  adulterers  and  drinks  with  them,  let  him  leave  10 
off  and  remove  from  the  place  of  the  unclean  and  wicked, 
or  he  shall  be  rejected.
