The  bishop  shall  bless  and  shall  not  be  blessed.  He 
shall  ordain  men  and  offer  the  Oblation;  and  the  Eulogia 
shall  be  offered  by  the  bishops  and  not  by  the  presbyters. 
The  bishop  shall  excommunicate  every  priest  who  is  20 
worthy  of  excommunication :  a  bishop  he  cannot  ex- 
communicate by  himself,  but  only  with  bishops  like  him.
