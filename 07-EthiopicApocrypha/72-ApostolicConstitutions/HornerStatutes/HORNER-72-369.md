Everyone   shall   hasten  to   bring  to  the   bishop  the  15 
first  of  the  fruits  of  his  crop,  and  the  bishop  also  shall 
take   them   and   bless  them,  and  he  shall  mention  the 
name  of  him  who  brought  them  to  him,  and  shall  say : 
We  thank  thee,  O  God,  and  we  bring  to  thee  the  first- 
fruit  of  the  fruits  which  thou  hast  given  us  to  receive  10 
of  them  and  hast  perfected  them  according  to  thy  word, 
and  commanded  the  earth  to  send  up  all  the  fruits  for 
the  joy  and  nourishment  of  men  and  all  living  creatures ; 
we  thank  thee,  O  God,  for  this  and  all  others  of  them 
by  which  we  are  benefited,  and  thou  hast  disposed  thy  25 
creation  with  variety  of  fruits,  through  thy  Son  Jesus 
Christ  our  Master,  by  whom  be  glory  to  thee  with  him 
and  the  Holy  Spirit  to  ages  of  ages,  Amin.
