And  let  not  the  bishop  exalt  himself  over  the  deacons 
and  presbyters,  nor  the  presbyters  over  the  people,  because 
the  standing  of  the  Church  depends  upon  one  another.  If 
there  were  no  lay  people,  over  whom  would  be  the  bishop  lo 
and  presbyter?  It  is  of  ourselves  that  we  all  become 
Christians,  viz.  Nazarenes,  but  as  for  becoming  apostles 
and  bishops  or  anything  else,  from  this  time  it  is  not  by 
ourselves  but  by  God  who  gives  the  gifts.
