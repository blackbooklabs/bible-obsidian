When  the  teacher  has  finished  the  instruction,  let 
the  catechumens  pray  by  themselves  and  separated  from 
the  Faithful.  And  let  the  women  stand  in  a  place  in  ; 
the  church  by  themselves,  the  Faithful  women  and  the 
female  catechumens.  And  when  they  have  finished  the 
prayer,  the  female  catechumens  shall  not  give  the  salu- 
tation, because  their  kiss  is  not  yet  pure,  but  let  the 
Faithful  women  kiss  one  another ;  the  men  shall  kiss  the  lo 
men,  and  the  women  shall  kiss  the  women,  and  the  men 
shall  not  kiss  the  women.  And  all  the  women  shall  veil 
their  heads  with  their  balalin  ^  or  their  mantles,  and  not 
with  linen  only,  because  this  is  not  suitable  for  them.
