When  thou  hast  blessed  the  cup  in  the  name  of  God 
and  receivest  of  it,  verily  it  is  the  Blood  of  Christ  Guard 
strictly  that  none  of  it  be  poured  away,  lest  the  alien  25 
spirits  lick  it  up  and  thou  be  one  who  hast  despised  and 
become  a  blasphemer*  of  the  Blood  of  Christ,  because 
thou  hast  despised  that  with  which  thou  wast  redeemed.
