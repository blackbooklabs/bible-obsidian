When  (a  widow)  is  appointed  she  shall  not  be  signed, 
but  shall  be  made  by  the  name.  If  her  husband  has  30 
been  dead  a  long  time  let  her  be  appointed.  If  her 
husband  has  lately  died  she  shall  not  be  trusted,  but  if 
she  had  become  aged  let  her  be  tested  for  a  time,  for 
solicitations  grow  old  in  htm  who  makes  place  for  them 
in  him.  Let  the  widow  be  appointed  with  the  word  35 
alone,  and  let  her  be  joined  to  the  rest  of  the  widows, 
and  hand  shall  not  be  placed  upon  her,  because  she 
does  not  offer  the  Oblations  nor  have  any  ministry.
