But  even  if  she  is  aged,  she  shall  be  tried  many  days, 
because  lust  will  contend  with  those  who  are  ordained  to  a 
place.  ^  And  the  widow  shall  be  ordained  by  word  only, 
and  she  shall  (then)  be  joined  to  the  rest  of  the  widows  ; 
and  they  shall  not  lay  hand  upon  her,  because  she  does 
not  offer  the  sacrifice,  nor  has  she  a  (sacred)  ministry. 
For  the  sealing  is  for  the  priests  because  of  their  ministry, 
but  (the  duty)  of  widows  is  about  prayer,  which  is  the 
duty  of  all.
