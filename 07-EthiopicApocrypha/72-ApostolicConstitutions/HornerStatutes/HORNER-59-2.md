The  deaconesses  shall  not  bless,  and  they  shall  not 
do  any  work  which  the  presbyters  and  deacons  do,  but 
they  shall  keep  the  gates  of  the  church.  Except  this 
25  there  is  no  other  work  which  they  do,  except  the 
ministering  to  the  presbyters  when  they  baptise  the 
women,  for  this  is  that  which  is  proper  for  them.
