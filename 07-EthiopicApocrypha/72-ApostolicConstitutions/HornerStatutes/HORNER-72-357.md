Concerning  that  it  is  proper  that  they  should  eat  with 
moderation  and  temperance  and  not  to  be  drunken. 
S  Ye  shall  eat  and  drink  orderly,  and  ye  shall  not  drink 
and  be  drunken,  lest  men  mock  you,  and  he  who  in- 
vited you  repent  himself  over  your  dissipation,  but  rather 
he  shall  be  praying  that  the  holy  ones  may  come  into  his 
house.     He  said  :  Ye  are  the  salt  of  the  earth.
