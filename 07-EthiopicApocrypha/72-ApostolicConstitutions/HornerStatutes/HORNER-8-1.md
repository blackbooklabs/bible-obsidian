Said  Ya'ekob :  O  my  son,  thou  shalt  not 
be  one  who  interprets  prodigies,  or  who  is  an  augur ; 
because  all  this  guides  to  idolatry.  Nor  shalt  thou  be  a 
wizard  or  a  charmer,  or  a  chooser  of  hours,  or  a  chooser 
of  days,  or  a  star-gazer,  or  a  magician,  or  of  men  who  deal 
y)  with  omens ;  nor  shalt  thou  desire  to  be  acquainted  with 
them  ;  because  all  this  is  for  thee  idolatry.
