If  there  was  not  a  law  of  difference  of  Orders,  it  would 
have  sufficed  to  perfect  all  the  world  with  one  name,  but 
since  we  have  been  taught  by  the  Lord  a  series  of 
functions  we  have  kept  apart  the  Bishops  for  the  chief  is 
priesthood,  and  the  Presbyters  for  the  priesthood,  and 
the  Deacons  for  the  service,  the  service  which  comes  to 
the  two,  that  the  works  of  the  service  may  be  perfected 
withal.
