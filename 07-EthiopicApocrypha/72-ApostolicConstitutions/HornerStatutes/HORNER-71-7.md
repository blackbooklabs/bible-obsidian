IS  shall  not  be  confused,  and  ye  shall  not  seize  for  your 
own  selves  an  ordination  which  was  not  given  to  you,  and 
ye  shall  not  pass  beyond  by  force  to  possess  that  which 
is  not  yours,  but  belongs  to  others,  over  which  ye  have 
no  authority.
