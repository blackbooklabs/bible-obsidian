Concerning  that  it  is  seemly  to  receive  of 
the  Eucharist  early  at  the  time  when  they  offer  (it)  up, 
90  before  that  they  taste  anything.  All  believers  shall 
accept  the  admonition  that  they  should  receive  of  the 
Mystery  before  that  they  taste  anything.  And  if  he  who 
receives  has  faith,  then  if  one  gave  to  him  deadly  poison 
it  shall  not  hurt  him.
