Concerning  male  Virgins.  They  shall  not  ordain  male 
virgins,  because  this  thing  is  from  the  will  of  the  heart 
alone  by  the  grace  of  God  and  by  the  love  of  Christ  s 
Jesus;  for  when  the  Holy  Spirit  makes  abode  with  a 
man,  he  obtains  grace  and  healing,  being  known 
by  the  grace  which  he  has,  which  will  be  known  to 
all  men.  And  if  he  had  a  call  for  this  work  (of 
the  ministry),  that  he  should  be  ordained  bishop  or  lo 
presbyter  or  deacon,  they  shall  lay  hand  upon  him 
\lit.  them).
