And Solomon the King wrote and sent a letter, saying, “Peace be to Baḷtasôr, the King of Rômê! Take my son ‘Adrâmî, and give him thy daughter, and make him king in the city of Rômê. Thou didst wish for a king of the seed of David my father, and I have done thy will. And I have sent unto thee his nobles, fourteen on his right hand and fourteen on his left, who shall keep the Law with him and be subject unto thee according to thy will.”
