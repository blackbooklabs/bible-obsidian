And the owner of the sheep came to the King and made an appeal to him, saying, “Give me back my sheep, for he hath carried them off because they went into his vineyard.” And the King said unto them, “Go ye and argue your case before your King ‘Adrâmî, and whatsoever he shall say unto you that do.” And they went and argued their case before him. And ‘Adrâmî asked him, saving, “How much of the vineyard have the sheep eaten? The leaves, or the tendrils, or the young grapes, or the shoots by the roots?”
