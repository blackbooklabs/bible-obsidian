## Verse ID Convention

All verses follow the deterministic format:

```
[ID]-[CHAPTER]-[VERSE]
```

**Examples:**
- `GEN-1-1` → Genesis 1:1
- `1ENOCH-1-1` → 1 Enoch 1:1
- `PSALM-1-1` → Psalm 1:1
- `MAT-1-1` → Matthew 1:1

**Edge Cases:**
- **Psalm 151** (Ethiopian addition): `PSA-151-1`
- **Daniel 13-14** (Additional chapters in Orthodox canon): `DAN-13-1`, `DAN-14-1`
- **Letter of Jeremiah** (Baruch 6): `BAR-6-1` (treated as chapter 6 of Baruch)
- **Multi-part apocrypha** (e.g., Testament of Isaac & Jacob): `TESTIJ-1-1` for Isaac, with internal numbering

## Notes on Canon Variants

### Ethiopian-81 Specifics

1. **Psalm 151**: Included as a full psalm (not an appendix)
2. **Daniel Extended**: Chapters 13-14 (Susanna and Bel & Dragon) fully integrated
3. **Esther Longer**: Ethiopian recension includes additions
4. **Ethiopic Apocrypha**: 1 Enoch, 2 Enoch, Jubilees, Psalms of Solomon carry full canonical weight
5. **Liturgical Texts**: Portions of hymnal and saints' lives carry scriptural authority in Ethiopian tradition

### Translation Reference

- **Primary Source**: Ge'ez (Classical Ethiopic) with English translations from scholarly editions (e.g., NETS, Oxford World's Classics)
- **Secondary Alignment**: NRSV, ESV, KJV where applicable to core Hebrew/Greek texts
- **Ethiopic-Specific**: Direct English translations from Ge'ez manuscripts for apocryphal texts
